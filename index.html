how & when to use me

01 Patch Test

Patch test a small amount of product on your jawline or behind the ear, overnight or for 24 hours. If you see no adverse reaction, you're good to go to use all over the face!

02 Ease in

Apply after cleansing your face or after your toner step. Start with introducing the product in your PM routine, and slowly work up in the AM routine as well. Follow up with a sunscreen in the AM and a moisturiser in your PM routine.

03 Enhance Results

Once your skin has adjusted to hustle (2 weeks period), you can start applying hustle on damp skin to improve the efficacy and results. Skin will feel plumper and absorption of the product will be better enhanced.
